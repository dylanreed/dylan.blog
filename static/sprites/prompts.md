# Pixel Art Concept Art Prompts

# SPRITES

## Health
- **Skeleton**: Small cartoon skeleton with glowing eye sockets, wearing a tattered brown cloak
- **Apothecary Cat**: Small witch's cat with glowing green eyes, wearing an apothecary satchel with potion bottles

## Tabletop
- **Mini Knight**: Tiny armored knight figurine with oversized helmet and wooden shield, tabletop miniature style
- **Dragon**: Small four-legged dragon whelp with stubby wings and curled tail, bright scales

## Clowning
- **Clown**: Small jovial clown with red nose, polka dot outfit, tiny hat, juggling balls in pocket
- **Balloon Dog**: Quadruped balloon animal dog with shiny reflective surface, twisted balloon segments

## Tech
- **Laptop Wizard**: Tiny hooded wizard carrying a miniature glowing laptop instead of a spellbook, pixel circuits on robe
- **Golem**: Small mechanical golem made of gears and copper parts, glowing blue eye visor, steam puffs

## Writing
- **Floating Quill**: Enchanted quill pen floating and writing by itself, trailing ink drops
- **Tiny Muse**: Small ethereal fairy-like muse with flowing robes, carrying a tiny scroll, glowing softly

## Food
- **Chef Imp**: Small mischievous kitchen imp with chef hat, wooden spoon, flour-dusted apron
- **Animated Cookpot**: Small walking cauldron with stubby legs, bubbling with colorful stew, steam rising

## Music
- **Tiny Bard**: Small wandering bard with oversized lute, feathered cap, colorful clothing
- **Walking Lute**: Enchanted lute with tiny legs, walking and playing itself, musical notes floating

## Travel
- **Miniature Adventurer**: Tiny explorer with oversized backpack, walking stick, worn boots, compass around neck
- **Pack Mule**: Small cartoon pack mule carrying saddlebags stuffed with supplies, rolled maps

## Reading
- **Floating Tome**: Enchanted book flying open with pages fluttering, glowing softly
- **Bookworm**: Cute cartoon bookworm with tiny spectacles, poking out of a leather-bound book

## Garden
- **Walking Plant**: Small potted plant with stubby root-legs, happy flower face, wandering cheerfully
- **Garden Sprite**: Tiny fairy-like garden spirit with leaf wings, carrying watering can, flower crown

## Craft
- **Animated Hammer**: Enchanted hammer with tiny legs, walking purposefully, sparks trailing
- **Crafting Sprite**: Small workshop gnome with goggles, leather apron, carrying gears and tools

## Default
- **Wandering Wizard**: Small robed wizard with pointy hat, long beard, walking staff with glowing gem
- **Tiny Knight**: Small armored knight with sword and shield, plume on helmet, walking patrol
- **Forest Bird**: Colorful small bird with long tail feathers, hopping and flying
- **Butterfly**: Bright magical butterfly with detailed wings, floating gently
