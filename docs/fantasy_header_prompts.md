## Style Notes

### Color Palette
- **Day mode**: Warm golds, rich browns, forest greens, cream/parchment tones, sunset oranges
- **Night mode**: Deep blues, silver moonlight, warm amber candlelight, purple shadows

### Key Visual Elements
- Thatched roofs and timber frame buildings
- Cobblestone and dirt paths
- Hanging wooden signs
- Candlelight and firelight
- Magical sparkles and glows
- Cozy, lived-in details

### Reference Games
- King's Quest series (Sierra)
- Quest for Glory series
- Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past
- Secret of Mana
- Chrono Trigger (village scenes)
- Stardew Valley (cozy aesthetic)
